Biography


LAS FROGAS is an interdisciplinary artist whose body of work draws from the memories of growing up on the unsupervised internet. her developing project focuses on her adulthood after trauma and the effect being present online at a young age had on her mental health. in her practice, LAS FROGAS combines her discipline in illustration with printmaking, painting, and animation to render these formative moments of her childhood to portray the mental strain that came with unsupervised exposure.

LAS FROGAS utilizes colorful, cute, and eye candy-esque style, and she foils these playful graphics with her strange blob frog-like creatures, the "FROGAS". she aims to emulate the experience of a childhood spent online through her art, combining both the nostalgic and distressing aspects of it.

LAS FROGAS holds a BFA in fine arts from Columbia College Chicago




Artist Statement


"My artistic practice revolves around the traumatic experiences I faced as a child as a direct consequence of using the internet without caution. Having grown up in the early to mid-2000’s, the online scene was an environment that harbored a rich artistic community; however, there was also a deeper evil which housed a biosphere of festering sin. People took advantage of hiding behind an online “persona” which rendered their real-life presence nearly anonymous. My main interest is in this relationship between a person and a screen, where a person splits in two, the person and the “persona”, with the former protected from the filth they indulge in online.


The manual labor of silkscreen printing juxtaposes the automated appearance of my pixelated and dithered artistic style, which represents the act of “processing”: thinking, performing, dealing with memory. I experiment with the technical definition of layering as being an act of concealing something deep, uprooting something to the forefront, and making them bleed into each other to create complex visuals. I have been interested in this relationship in reference to my own mental state since childhood, and my journey of “processing” and growth.


Experimenting with digital illustration, printmaking, and layering, my work renders my growth into adulthood past trauma and this concept of the “persona” together. At the forefront of my artwork are the strange blob frog-like creatures, the "FROGAS", my own “persona”, and a deconstruction of myself into a complex of memory and emotion. The “FROGAS” foil with cutesy, eye candy-esque environments, indicating a sense of a deeper and sinister presence. This style is easy, fun, and approachable; it is designed to appeal to a general audience and later instill unease when engaged with." - LAS FROGAS
